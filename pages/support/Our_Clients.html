Four Texas Businesses · Five Engagements

NEXUS Security serves Texas businesses across multiple industries. Each client has unique challenges, regulatory requirements, and stakeholders. You’ll work with all four over the course of the semester.

Brazos Financial Group — Modules 1 & 4

Dallas, Texas • Financial Services • 450 employees

Wealth management firm managing $2.8B in assets. A recent merger brought legacy systems and compliance concerns. An SEC examination is scheduled in 6 months. Leadership knows they have problems but needs data to justify remediation budget.

Environment: Windows Active Directory   •   Key Issue: SEC compliance

Key Stakeholders:

  • Ray Jimenez — CTO
  • Victoria Caldwell — CCO
  • Maggie Brazos-Chen — CEO
Gulf Coast Healthcare Partners — Modules 2 & 3

Houston, Texas • Healthcare • 800+ employees

Multi-location healthcare network with 12 clinics across Greater Houston. The patient portal has certificate issues causing browser security warnings. HIPAA compliance is non-negotiable — patient trust is everything.

Environment: Mixed (Linux servers)   •   Key Issue: HIPAA compliance, patient trust

Key Stakeholders:

  • Dr. Michael Okonkwo — CMO
  • Jennifer Walsh — CISO
  • Thomas Kowalski — IT Manager
LoneStar DevOps — Modules 3 & 5

Austin, Texas • Technology • 45 employees

Fast-growing DevOps startup seeking enterprise clients. Series B funding depends on passing enterprise security reviews. A “move fast, break things” culture is colliding with enterprise compliance requirements.

Environment: Cloud-native, containers   •   Key Issue: Enterprise security review
