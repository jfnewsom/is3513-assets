You’ve just been hired as a Junior Security Analyst. NEXUS specializes in helping Texas businesses understand and improve their security posture. Your first day starts now.

How Engagements Work

Every module follows the same consulting workflow. Internal training first, then a client engagement at the end.

  • Labs X.1–X.2 — Foundation Labs (Internal Training): Before touching a client system, you train on NEXUS’s methodology. Senior analysts guide you through tools, techniques, and documentation standards.
  • Lab X.3 — Engagement Packet (Client Engagement): You’re on the clock. Apply your training to a real client’s systems, document your findings, and deliver a professional report that goes to executives.
  • Deliverable — Engagement Packet: One .docx file, two parts. Part I (Internal Packet): Timesheet + Runbook + Reflection. Part II (Client Deliverable): Cover, Executive Summary, Findings, Risk Analysis, Recommendations. The client only sees Part II. See the Engagement Packet Guide for details.
Red Team, Blue Team, Purple Team

Real security work demands both offensive and defensive perspectives. Red team thinking is the attacker’s mindset — what could go wrong, how would they get in, where are the gaps. Blue team thinking is the defender’s — what controls catch this, how do we detect, how do we respond. The course rotates you through both.

Modules 2 and 5 sit in the middle — the purple team zone where you find the weakness AND recommend the fix. That’s the consulting sweet spot.

Red Team Thinking
Modules 1 & 4 — you operate from the attacker’s side.

Your Responsibilities as a Junior Analyst
  • Track Your Time: Every hour is billable — internal training or client work. Your timesheet justifies NEXUS’s invoices.
  • Document Everything: If it’s not documented, it didn’t happen. Your runbooks help the next analyst; your reports go to executives.
  • Communicate Professionally: Clients aren’t always technical. Translate findings into business impact and make recommendations actionable.
  • Learn Continuously: Every module builds on the last. Skills compound. By the end, you’ll have a portfolio of five professional engagement packets.
