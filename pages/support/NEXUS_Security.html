How Engagements Work

Every module follows the same consulting workflow. Internal training first, then a client engagement at the end.

  • Weeks 1–2 — Internal Training: Before touching a client system, you train on NEXUS’s methodology. Senior analysts guide you through tools, techniques, and documentation standards.
  • Week 3 — Client Engagement: You’re on the clock. Apply your training to a real client’s systems, document your findings, and deliver a professional report that goes to executives.
  • Deliverable — Engagement Packet: Section A (Timesheet), Section B (Internal Runbook), Section C (Client Report). Format: .docx only.

Your Responsibilities as a Junior Analyst
  • Track Your Time: Every hour is billable — internal training or client work. Your timesheet justifies NEXUS’s invoices.
  • Document Everything: If it’s not documented, it didn’t happen. Your runbooks help the next analyst; your reports go to executives.
  • Communicate Professionally: Clients aren’t always technical. Translate findings into business impact and make recommendations actionable.
  • Learn Continuously: Every module builds on the last. Skills compound. By the end, you’ll have a portfolio of five real consulting engagements.
