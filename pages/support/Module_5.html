Client Context: LoneStar DevOps (Austin, TX)
LoneStar is an 85-person SaaS startup with $12M ARR and a Series B on the horizon. Their infrastructure was set up by a developer who left six months ago. An enterprise prospect requires a third-party security assessment before signing. Amanda Nguyen (CEO) needs a report she can hand to Longhorn Ventures investors. Sarah Martinez (Senior DevOps) needs a roadmap she can actually execute with two engineers and no staging environment.

“Longhorn Ventures wants a security report before they wire the Series B. I need confidence statements and timeframes I can actually defend in a board meeting — not a list of CVEs.”

Amanda Nguyen
CEO & Co-Founder, LoneStar DevOps

“I’ve got two engineers, no staging environment, and a developer who left six months ago took half the institutional knowledge with him. Give me a remediation roadmap I can actually execute.”

Sarah Martinez
Senior DevOps Engineer, LoneStar DevOps
