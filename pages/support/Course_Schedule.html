How Module 5 Differs

Module 5 is the only module that runs as 2 units instead of 3. Lab 5.1 merges what would have been two separate Foundation Labs (vulnerability discovery and risk analysis) into a single longer lab. Lab 5.2 is the final Engagement Packet.

Lab 5.1 keeps the same Friday deadline (Day 5) as every other Lab X.1 — only the scope is larger. There is no Wednesday Lab X.2 deadline in Module 5; that time becomes additional work time for the Engagement Packet.

Key Dates

First Day: Tue May 26  •  Last Day: Tue Aug 4  •  Final Grades Due: Fri Aug 7

University holidays: Mon May 25 (Memorial Day) · Fri Jun 19 (Emancipation Day) · Sat Jul 4 (Independence Day). Summer is compressed; no holiday accommodations — work ahead if a holiday will affect you.

Registrar deadlines: Census Wed Jun 10 · Drop Deadline Wed Jul 8 · Withdrawal Deadline Fri Jul 31

Foundation Lab deadlines are Fri/Wed 11:59 PM CT. Engagement Packets are due Sun 11:59 PM CT. Module exams are open Sun – Tue. No late work accepted — use drop policies for emergencies.

Drop Policies

One drop per category, framed as built-in sick days:

• 1 of 9 Foundation Labs (Lab 1.1 droppable but strongly discouraged — it sets up your working environment)
• 1 of 5 Engagement Packets
• 1 of 5 Module Exams

Just like at work, sick days are not unlimited. Take more than one drop here and it comes out of your GPA in the form of forfeited grades.
