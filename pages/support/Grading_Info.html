Two-Part Grading for Engagement Packets
Engagement Packets use additive scoring (Quality Ladder) plus documentation deductions. You earn points for quality work, then lose points for missing citations and screenshots.
Part 1: Quality Ladder (Additive)
Component Weight What We Evaluate
Assignment Objectives 40% All three weeks complete? Timesheet, Runbook, Client Report present?
Technical Documentation 40% Reproducibility — could another analyst follow your runbook?
Professional Communication 20% Client-ready? Clear writing, proper formatting, executive summary?
Part 2: Documentation Deductions
-10 pts
Per Missing Citation
10 citations required. Zero citations = -100 points. This is not optional.
-5 pts
Per Missing/Unverifiable Screenshot
Screenshots must show your VM name. No VM name = unverifiable = deduction.
Why deductions? In professional security work, undocumented claims are worthless. No evidence, no finding. These requirements ensure you build professional habits from day one.
