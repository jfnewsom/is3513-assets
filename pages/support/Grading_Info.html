How Your Work Is Evaluated

Your grade reflects professional competency, not just completion. We use a two-part system for Engagement Packets: additive scoring for quality, plus deductions for missing documentation requirements.

Part 2 — Documentation Deductions
−5% per missing citation (10 required; capped at −50%)
−2.5% per missing screenshot (20 required; a screenshot without the VM name visible counts as missing; capped at −50%)
These are not optional. In professional security work, undocumented claims are worthless.
Drop Policies
1 Foundation Lab grade dropped (lowest of 9)
1 Engagement Packet grade dropped (lowest of 5)
1 Module Exam grade dropped (lowest of 5)
Think of drops as built-in sick days: Just like at work, sick days are not unlimited. Depending on how long you've been with the company, you get more — but in this class, you only have one per category. Take more than one day at work and it comes out of your check in the form of forfeited wages. Take more than one drop here and it comes out of your GPA in the form of forfeited grades.
Late Work Policy: No late work accepted. Use your drop — that’s what it’s for.
Questions about grading? Ask in #course-questions on Discord. Document everything — evidence is everything in this field.
