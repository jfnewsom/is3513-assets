Your grade reflects professional competency, not just completion. We use a two-part system for Engagement Packets: additive scoring for quality, plus deductions for missing documentation requirements.

Drop Policies
2 Foundation Lab grades dropped (lowest)
1 Engagement Packet grade dropped (lowest)
1 Module Exam grade dropped (lowest)
Drop policies exist for emergencies. Use them wisely — don’t burn a drop on procrastination.
Late Work Policy: No late work accepted. The drop policies exist to accommodate emergencies, illness, and life events. If something comes up, a drop covers it — you don’t need a separate exception.
