Part 2 — Documentation Deductions
−5% per missing citation (10 required; capped at −50%)
−2.5% per missing screenshot (20 required; a screenshot without the VM name visible counts as missing; capped at −50%)
Citations must meet the source quality standard: NIST, OWASP, RFCs, tool documentation, man pages. Wikipedia, blogs, and tutorial sites do not count toward the 10. See the Citations page for the full rule.
These are not optional. In professional security work, undocumented claims are worthless.

Think of drops as built-in sick days

Just like at work, sick days are not unlimited. Depending on how long you've been with the company, you get more — but in this class, you only have one per category. Take more than one day at work and it comes out of your check in the form of forfeited wages. Take more than one drop here and it comes out of your GPA in the form of forfeited grades.

block
Late Work Policy

No late work accepted. If an emergency makes you miss a deadline, your one drop in that category absorbs it. Plan accordingly — drops are scarce, so save them for real emergencies.

Questions about grading? Ask in #course-questions on Discord. Document everything — evidence is everything in this field.
