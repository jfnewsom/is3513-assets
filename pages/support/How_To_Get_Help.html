1. Ask on Discord (Fastest Response)

The class Discord is the fastest way to get help. Post your question in #lab-help or #course-questions and classmates or the instructor will respond—often within minutes.

When asking questions:

• Describe what you're trying to do
• Share the error message (use code blocks!)
• Include a screenshot if relevant
• Be specific—"it doesn't work" doesn't help us help you
