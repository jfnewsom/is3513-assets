Virtual Machine Configuration

Your Kali Linux VM is your security workstation for the entire semester. This page covers quick-reference info—Lab 1.1 has the full step-by-step instructions.

Officially Supported: VirtualBox with Kali Linux installed from ISO. Other hypervisors (VMware, Parallels, UTM) may work but you’re responsible for your own troubleshooting.

VM Naming Convention (Critical!)

Your VM must be named following this format—it appears in your terminal prompt and verifies your work is yours:

kali-lastname-studentID

Example: kali-smith-abc123
