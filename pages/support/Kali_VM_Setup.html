VM Naming Convention (Critical!)

Your VM must be named following this format—it appears in your terminal prompt and verifies your work is yours:

kali-lastname-studentID

Example: kali-smith-abc123

Recommended VM Specs
RAM: 4 GB minimum
CPU: 2 cores minimum
Disk: 40 GB (dynamically allocated)
Network: NAT (default)
Video: 128 MB VRAM
Display: Enable 3D if available
