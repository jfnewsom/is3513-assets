Client Context: Gulf Coast Healthcare Partners
Gulf Coast moved their patient portal to Azure (hosted by LoneStar DevOps) after the ransomware incident 18 months ago. With an OCR audit approaching, Jennifer Walsh (CISO) wants independent validation of the network traffic patterns to and from the portal. Thomas Kowalski (VP Infrastructure) made the call to move to cloud — he wants to see it was the right one.

“The OCR auditors will ask about our cloud traffic. I need evidence that we know what’s flowing to Azure — not assumptions, evidence.”

Jennifer Walsh
