How to Use the Textbook
  • Before each module: Read the assigned chapters to understand the concepts you’ll be practicing in the labs.
  • During labs: Reference specific sections when you need deeper explanation of a technique or tool.
  • For reports: Cite the textbook when explaining methodology or referencing security principles. It counts toward your 10-citation requirement.
  • For exams: Module exams draw from both textbook content and lab experience.

“If we don’t touch it in a lab, we don’t assign the reading.” Every assigned chapter directly supports the hands-on work.

The textbook is your reference manual. The labs are where you learn.
