Our Reading Philosophy
"If we don't touch it in a lab, we don't assign the reading." Every assigned chapter directly supports the hands-on work. Read the chapters before starting each module's labs — they provide the conceptual foundation you'll apply.
How to Use the Textbook

Before each module: Read the assigned chapters to understand the concepts you'll be practicing.

During labs: Reference specific sections when you need deeper explanation of a technique or tool.

For reports: Cite the textbook when explaining methodology or referencing security principles.

For exams: The module exams draw from both textbook content and lab experience.

The textbook is your reference manual. The labs are where you learn.
