Welcome to NEXUS Security
You've just been hired as a Junior Security Analyst at NEXUS Security, a boutique cybersecurity consulting firm based in San Antonio, Texas. Your first day starts now.

NEXUS Security specializes in helping Texas businesses understand and improve their security posture. Our clients range from regional financial institutions to healthcare organizations to manufacturing companies preparing for federal compliance. We're small—which means you'll get hands-on experience with real engagements from day one.

How Engagements Work
WEEKS 1-2
Internal Training
Before you touch a client system, you train on NEXUS's methodology. Senior analysts guide you through tools, techniques, and documentation standards.
WEEK 3
Client Engagement
You're on the clock. Apply your training to a real client's systems, document your findings, and deliver a professional report that goes to executives.
Purple Team Operations

At NEXUS, we don't believe in artificial boundaries between offense and defense. Every engagement considers both perspectives:

Red Team Thinking:
How would an attacker approach this? What would they find? What would they exploit?
Blue Team Thinking:
How do we detect this? How do we prevent it? What controls actually work?
Your Responsibilities
Track Your Time: Every hour is billable—internal training or client work. Your timesheet justifies NEXUS's invoices.
Document Everything: If it's not documented, it didn't happen. Your runbooks help the next analyst. Your reports go to executives.
Communicate Professionally: Clients aren't technical. Translate findings into business impact. Make recommendations actionable.
Learn Continuously: Every module builds on the last. Skills compound. By the end, you'll have a portfolio of real consulting work.
